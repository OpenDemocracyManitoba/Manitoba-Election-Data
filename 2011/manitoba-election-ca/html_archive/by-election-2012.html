Fort Whyte 2012 By-Election

Official Map

This constituency is located in the southwest portion of Winnipeg. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 19,200. This area was previously represented by Hugh McFadyen (Progressive Conservative Party) until July 30th, 2012. The seat remained vacant until the by-election was called on August 3rd, 2012.

Daniella Ponticelli, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
