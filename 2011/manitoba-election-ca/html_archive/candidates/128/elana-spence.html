Questionnaire Response

Elana Spence - Lac du Bonnet

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

My interest in politics is rooted in the idea of public service and duty to the people I would represent. It is my goal to give the people of Lac du Bonnet a strong voice in the provincial legislature.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I am an honest, hardworking and dedicated person committed to being accessible to all people within the constituency

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Maintain and improve the level of healthcare, more opportunities for young people, access to clean water and affordable energy.
