Questionnaire Response

Dan Manning - River Heights

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

I know what brings young people to Manitoba and it is the forward movement of the NDP that has made Manitoba attractive and stronger for families.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

Married to a life-long River Heights girl and know the issues facing our community. A trusted advocate, I can effectively bring concerns to government and make change.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Daycare accessibility, education funding, maintaining the arts and music community, supporting our students, keeping our lakes healthy and province green.
