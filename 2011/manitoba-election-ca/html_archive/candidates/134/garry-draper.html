Questionnaire Response

Garry Draper - Arthur-Virden

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

I am running because I see a need for a strong voice to represent the families living in Arthur-Virden. I am the voice that will make sure their concerns are addressed, and their priorities are kept at the forefront.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I was born and raised here and now my two sons have started farming along side me. I understand the issues that face our community and our young people.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Dependable health care now and for the future, providing more educational opportunities, agriculture and the dismantling of the CWB and flooding.
