Questionnaire Response

Rosemary Hnatiuk - Lakeside

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

I want to effectively advocate for the changes and improvements for health care, child and family services.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

Born and raised in the central Interlake, I know the area and know the issues that face it.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Jobs, economic development and health care.
