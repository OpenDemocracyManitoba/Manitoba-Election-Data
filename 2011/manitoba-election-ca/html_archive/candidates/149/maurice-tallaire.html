Questionnaire Response

Maurice Tallaire - La Verendrye

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

I am running because I love my community, and feel I can help move it forward. I will give all my constituents fair and equal representation.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I am a people person. I share the same values as others in my community. I understand what they need, and will work to address those needs.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Education opportunities, continued access to health care and internet service.
