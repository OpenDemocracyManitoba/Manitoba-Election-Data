Questionnaire Response

Aaron McDowell - Morden-Winkler

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

To represent the wonderful, hardworking people of Mordon-Winkler, and give them a strong voice.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

Since I was born and raised in rural Manitoba, I have insight into the priorities and concerns of families.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Health care, education & affordability.
