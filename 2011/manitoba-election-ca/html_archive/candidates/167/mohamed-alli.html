Questionnaire Response

Mohamed Alli - Morris

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

I am running because I am confident to give the Constituency the best representation in Government.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I decided to run because I feel that I am the best-suited person to represent the constituents of Morris. As a former candidate in the last two Federal Elections for the Federal NDP, I understand the Issues constituents in the area have.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Compensation for flooded areas, crop damages and the dismantling of the Canadian Wheat Board.
