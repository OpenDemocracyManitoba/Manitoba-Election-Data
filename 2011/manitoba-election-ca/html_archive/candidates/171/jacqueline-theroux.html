Questionnaire Response

Jacqueline Theroux - Midland

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

Health care is the NDP’s top priority and that’s mine too. I believe if we work together we can continue to make steps in the right direction. A conservative government would risk all of our progress to date.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I've lived in the area for over 35 years. I know the people, I know the issues and I will work for everyone.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Attracting youth to rural Manitoba, health care and education.
