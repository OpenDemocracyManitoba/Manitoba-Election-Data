Questionnaire Response

Cynthia Ryan - St. Paul

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

An NDP government keeps the priorities that matter to families, just like mine, at top of mind. I want residents in this area to feel confident that they have someone working on their side and someone that will listen to their concerns.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I think my experience as a frontline healthcare provider has given me the experience of getting to know people and getting to know what they care about. I am interested in a better future to make St. Paul an ever greater place to call home.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Road infrastructure, education, more day care spaces, quality and affordable post secondary education, and affordable housing for seniors.
