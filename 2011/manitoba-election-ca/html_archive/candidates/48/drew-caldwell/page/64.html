Questionnaire Response

Drew Caldwell - Brandon East

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

I am running to continue building a better Brandon. It is an honor to have the respect of my community, and speaking up for them is something I take pride in

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I have a proven record of strong representation for Brandon. As MLA I have been able to deliver on major commitments including the Brandon Regional Health Centre, Assiniboine Community College, Brandon University and more affordable housing.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Healthcare, education, affordable housing, infrastructure and community safety.
