Questionnaire Response

Melanie Wight - Burrows

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

We’ve had a strong voice for our community, and I want to continue to keep moving in the right direction.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I have lived the past 20 years in the North End. I understand our issues. I care about all the very diverse people in our area. I will work hard for everyone.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Health care, programming for children, affordability for families & seniors and community safety
