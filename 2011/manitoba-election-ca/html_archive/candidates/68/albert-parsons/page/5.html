Questionnaire Response

Albert Parsons - Riding Mountain

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

I believe the province, under the NDP, is on the right track. I don't want to see the Tories turn the clock back and watch our province go backwards.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I have lived most of my adult life in the constituency, and I know the area well. I am in daily contact with the residents so I will represent them well.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Declining rural population, access to health care, education and recreational facilities.
