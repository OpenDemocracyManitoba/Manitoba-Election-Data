Questionnaire Response

Erna Braun - Rossmere

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

I wish to continue to serve my community and to provide a strong voice for the northeast corner of Winnipeg. It has been a pleasure to work with a variety of groups and individuals and find ways to support their efforts in our community.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

Growing up and living in the community has given me firsthand knowledge of the community, its citizens and their needs.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Education, healthcare and active transportation.
