Questionnaire Response

Cory Szczepanski - Spruce Woods

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

Our community needs a strong voice that keeps the focus on the things families care about.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I’m straight forward and enjoy working with people. I'm full of energy and want to help solve issues. I’m interested in a strong rural Manitoba for generations to come.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Sustaining rural Manitoba, opportunities for young people, good jobs, access to health care close to home & good roads and bridges.
