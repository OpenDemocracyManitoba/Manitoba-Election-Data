Questionnaire Response

Frank Whitehead - The Pas

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

I believe northern Manitoba plays an important part in shaping our province. It is a very diverse region of the province. An NDP government can continue to move the region forward.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I have several years of experience in community development and leadership as a community leader, negotiations and excellent relationships with all levels of governments and industry.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Health care and infrastructure.
