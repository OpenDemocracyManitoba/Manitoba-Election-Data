Questionnaire Response

Daryl Reid - Transcona

Why did you decide to run in the 2011 Manitoba election?

I’m running to continue to build services and supports for the families in Transcona.

What experience will you bring to compliment your role as MLA?

I am experienced, dedicated, and committed to the priorities of Transcona residents. An NDP government will continue to invest in strengthening communities like ours.

Is there anything in particular you would like to change in our province? In your electoral division?

Seniors housing, healthy living and recreation & daycare spaces.
