Fort Whyte

Official Map

This constituency is located in the southwest portion of Winnipeg. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 19,200. It is largely unchanged from the previous constituency of the same name; however, it no longer includes the area east of Waverly Street or south of Cadboro Road. This area was previously represented by Hugh McFadyen (Progressive Conservative Party) was first elected in 2005.

Daniella Ponticelli, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
