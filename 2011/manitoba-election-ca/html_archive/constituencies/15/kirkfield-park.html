Kirkfield Park

Official Map

This constituency is located in the western tip of Winnipeg. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 20,400. The only difference since its previous boundaries is that the constituency now extends an additional two blocks to the west from Vimy Road to Parkdale Street on the north side of Portage Ave. This area was previously represented by Sharon Blady (New Democratic Party) who was first elected in 2007.

Allison Marinelli, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
