Logan

Official Map

This constituency is located in the northwest portion of Winnipeg. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 20,900. It is a new constituency created with portions of the former Wellington, Minto, Point Douglas, and Fort Rouge constituencies. The constituency includes all neighbourhoods west of McPhillips Street, east of Main Street, south of the CPR tracks, and north of Notre Dame Ave. It also includes the core downtown area and the Centennial neighbourhood between Langside Street, Notre Dame Ave, and Ellice Ave. This area was previously represented by Flor Marcelino (New Democratic Party) in Wellington since 2007; by George Hickes (New Democratic Party) in Point Douglas since 1990; by Andrew Swan (New Democratic Party) in Minto since 2004; and by Jennifer Howard (New Democratic Party) in Fort Rouge since 2007.

Crystal Laderas, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
