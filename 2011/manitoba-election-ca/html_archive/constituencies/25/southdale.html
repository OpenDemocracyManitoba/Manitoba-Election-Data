Southdale

Official Map

This constituency is located in the southeast corner of Winnipeg. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 19,200. Its boundaries are largely unchanged, except that it no longer includes the portion east of Laigmodiere Blvd and north of Bishop Grandin Blvd or the portion north of Fermor Ave., both of which are now in Radisson. This area was previously represented by Erin Selby (New Democratic Party), who was first elected in 2007.

Garrick Kozier, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
