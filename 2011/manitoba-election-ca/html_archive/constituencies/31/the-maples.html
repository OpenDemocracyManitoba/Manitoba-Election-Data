The Maples

Official Map

This constituency is located in the northwest portion of Winnipeg. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 20,400. The new boundaries now include the northern part of the former Inkster constituency and no longer include the southern portion of the previous Maples constituency, which is now part of Burrows. The area was previously represented by Mohinder Saran (New Democratic Party) in The Maples since 2007 and by Kevin Lamoureux (Liberal Party) in Inkster since 2003.

Ashley Wiebe, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
