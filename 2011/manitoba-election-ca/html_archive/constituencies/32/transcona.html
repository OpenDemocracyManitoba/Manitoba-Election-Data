Transcona

Official Map

This constituency is located in the easternmost tip of Winnipeg. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 20,500. It is mostly unchanged from the previous constituency of the same name. This area was previously represented by Daryl Reid (New Democratic Party), who was first elected in 1990.
