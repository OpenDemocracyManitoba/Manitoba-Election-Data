Tuxedo

Official Map

This constituency is located in the west side of Winnipeg. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 20,300. It is largely unchanged from the previous constituency of the same name. This area was previously represented by Heather Stefanson (Progressive Conservative party), who was first elected in 2000.
