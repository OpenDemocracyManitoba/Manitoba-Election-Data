Wolseley

Official Map

This constituency is located in the central portion of Winnipeg. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 20,900. It is largely unchanged from the previous constituency of the same name. This area was previously represented by Rob Altemeyer of the New Democratic Party first elected in 2003.
