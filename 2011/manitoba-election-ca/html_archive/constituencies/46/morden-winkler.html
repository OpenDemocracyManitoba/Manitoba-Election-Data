Morden-Winkler

Official Map

This constituency is located in the southern region of Manitoba. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 19,500. It is a new constituency that includes the northeast corner of the former Pembina constituency. This area was previously represented by Peter George Dyck (Progressive Conservative Party) who was first elected in 1995.

Emily Wessel, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
