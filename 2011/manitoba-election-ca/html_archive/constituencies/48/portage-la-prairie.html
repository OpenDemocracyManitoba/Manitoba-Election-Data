Portage la Prairie

Official Map

This constituency is located in the south central portion of Manitoba. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 20,500. It is largely unchanged from the previous constituency of the same name. This area was previously represented by David Faurschou (Progressive Conservative Party) who was first elected in 1997.

Laura Kunzelman, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
