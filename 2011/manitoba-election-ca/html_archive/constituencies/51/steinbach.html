Steinbach

Official Map

This constituency is located in the southern portion of Manitoba. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 19,400. The new constituency boundaries no longer include a large portion to the south, which is now a part of La Verendrye, and a portion in the northwest, which is now part of Morris. This area was previously represented by Kelvin Goertzen (Progressive Conservative Party) who was first elected in 2003.

Lindsey Enns, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
