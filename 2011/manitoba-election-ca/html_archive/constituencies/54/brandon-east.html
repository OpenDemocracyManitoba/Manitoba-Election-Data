Brandon East

Official Map

This constituency is located in the eastern half of Brandon. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 20,700. It is largely unchanged from the previous constituency of the same name, with the addition of the block between Kirkcaldy Drive and Braecrest Drive. This area was previously represented by Drew Caldwell (New Democratic Party) who was first elected in 1999.

Daniella Ponticelli, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
