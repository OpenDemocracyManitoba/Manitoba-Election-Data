Brandon West

Official Map

This constituency is located in the western half of Brandon. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 20,800. The constituency lost a portion of its northeast corner to Brandon East, and gained a small portion in the southeast corner since the previous boundaries. This area was previously represented by Rick Borotsik (Progressive Conservative Party) who was first elected in 2007.

Daniella Ponticelli, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
