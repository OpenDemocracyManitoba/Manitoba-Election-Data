Dauphin

Official Map

This constituency is located in the western portion of Manitoba. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 19,600. This constituency has similar boundaries as the previous constituency of Dauphin-Roblin except that it no longer has the portion north of Dauphin Lake. This constituency was previously represented by Stan Struthers (New Democratic Party) who was first elected in 1995.

Lindsey Peterson, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
