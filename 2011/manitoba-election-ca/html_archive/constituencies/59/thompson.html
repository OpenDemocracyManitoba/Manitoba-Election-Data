Thompson

Official Map

This constituency is located in the northern portion of Manitoba. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 18,900. It is largely unchanged from the previous constituency, although the western-most portion of the previous constituency is now a part of Flin Flon. This area was previously represented by Steve Ashton (New Democratic Party) who was first elected in 1981.

Dani Finch, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
