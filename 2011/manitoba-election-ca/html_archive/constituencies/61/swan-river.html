Swan River

Official Map

This constituency is located in the western portion of Manitoba. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 19,400. It is largely unchanged from the previous constituency of the same name. This area was previously represented by Rosann Wowchuk (New Democratic Party) who was first elected in 1990.
