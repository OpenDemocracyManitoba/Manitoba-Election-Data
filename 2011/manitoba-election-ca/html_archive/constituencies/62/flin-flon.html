Flin Flon

Official Map

This constituency is located in the northwest portion of Manitoba. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 15,300. It is largely unchanged from the previous constituency of the same name. This area was previously represented by Gerard Jennissen (New Democratic Party) who was first elected in 1995.

Lindsey Peterson, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
