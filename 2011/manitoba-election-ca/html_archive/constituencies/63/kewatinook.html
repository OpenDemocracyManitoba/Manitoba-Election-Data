Kewatinook

Official Map

This constituency is the largest in Manitoba, sprawling over the northeastern and east regions of Manitoba. The approximate population of the constituency in 2008 was 15,600. It is a new constituency that has had little change from its predecessor, Rupertsland. This area was previously represented by Eric Robinson (New Democratic Party) who has been elected since 1993.

Sara Harrison, Creative Communications Student, Red River College
