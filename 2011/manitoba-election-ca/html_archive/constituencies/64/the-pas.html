The Pas

Official Map

This constituency is located in the northern portion of Manitoba. The population of the constituency in 2008 was 20,500. It is largely unchanged from the previous constituency of the same name. This area was previously represented by Frank Whitehead (New Democratic Party) who was first elected in 2009.
