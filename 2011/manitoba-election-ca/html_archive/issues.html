Issue Website Criteria

The criteria used to find and list these issue sites can be found in our FAQ.

If you have created or are aware of an election issue website that is not featured here, please bring it to our attention by contacting us.

ManitobaElection.ca will not post websites that promote violence, hatred, or intolerance towards any identifiable group or person. We will not post websites that are the product of a registered political party.
